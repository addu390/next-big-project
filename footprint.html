Ecological Footprint


Let's talk about our ecological footprint and how to reduce our footprint here on Earth. Being a part of this system, the very existence, the western world, we tend to leave behind a huge footprint without any easy options to reduce it.

While the bigger goal is to change the way we live. A good start would be to use cleaner transportation, use renewable sources of energy to your house and cultivate energy-saving habits, reduce your food footprint, and, most importantly, save as much water as you can!

I will write a different blog post and add references to good content out there for best practices, but in this post, I want to specifically focus on what companies around me can do to support this cause.

Amazon

On the right is a screen from the Amazon IOS application to choose a delivery speed after the checkout screen and delivery address selection.

I often see an option to choose a later date, along with a cashback, most likely - this is because there's already a package to be delivered on a later date. If that is true, the customer did contribute to reducing the carbon footprint of Amazon.

Rather than tracking an individual's carbon footprint, what if we can track the carbon footprint reduced by an individual. On the left is the screen I suggest, where the customer is appreciated for reducing the carbon footprint, a badge and score along with it would be a bonus, and maybe a CASHBACK too?

OLA Cabs

On the left is the OLA Cabs IOS application home screen

On to the right is an example illustration for emphasizing the carbon footprint score; I've informed the customer that picking a "mini" at this point of time would be ideal for reducing carbon footprint as compared to "Prime Sedan" (Would be of a lower score or a badge) - A higher score translating to a lower carbon footprint can be the other way around.

Clearly, this seems unfair for Sedan and SVN drivers, but giving a score depends on several aspects such as mileage, time to reach the pick-up point, number of people, availability, among others.


This applies to a lot of Companies out there, to name a few:
Micro mobility - Yulu
Bike Rentals - Bounce & Vogo
Delivery - Dunzo, Zomato, Swiggy
Ride Sharing - OLA, Rapido & QuickRide

What if all of the scores and badges are maintained by a common service provider, allowing the end user to visualise the overall reduction in carbon footprint.
